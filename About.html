Our Story

The Genesis

AIDA's journey began with a simple but ambitious goal: to disrupt the debt recovery industry and offer a fresh perspective on debt recovery. We started as a small team of debt recovery experts, who noticed a gap in the market. Traditional debt recovery methods weren't always effective, and often left businesses with unpaid debts and little hope of recovery. Determined to change this, we set out to create a startup that would leverage technology and innovation to deliver better results for our clients. We knew that we had to create a solution that was faster, more efficient, and more flexible than what was currently available.

The Growth

We started by building a team of experienced debt collectors who had the skills and knowledge to handle any type of debt, no matter how complex. We also invested in cutting-edge technology and developed proprietary software that allowed us to streamline our processes and recover debts faster. As we began to gain traction, we focused on delivering exceptional customer service, and building strong relationships with our clients. Our dedication to ethical practices, transparency, and innovation quickly set us apart from our competitors, and we soon became the go-to debt recovery company for businesses across various industries.
